Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to provide seasoned campers and new rafters alike with the best experience they can have. If you're camping out in the stars, or staying in a cabin, we provide the best equipment, saftey, and advice about river rafting.

Audience

We love to serve those who love camping, hiking, biking, and especially river rafting.

Q: What region do you serve?

A: We serve all of the Pacific Northwest, including regions in Washington, Oregon, and Idaho.

Q: What type of equipment do you sell?

A: We sell helmets, arm and knee pads, chin straps, rafts, oars, life vests, and food supplies such as trail mix, water, etc.

Q: What time of year are you open?

A: We are open during the spring until the fall. We are closed for the winter.
